Common COVID-19 Symptoms

  • Fever or chills - Often the first sign of infection
  • Persistent cough - Usually dry, may develop into productive cough
  • Shortness of breath - Can range from mild to severe
  • Fatigue - Extreme tiredness lasting days or weeks
  • Loss of taste or smell - May be partial or complete

⚠️ When to Seek Immediate Medical Care

  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Persistent chest pain or pressure
  • Confusion or inability to stay awake
  • Blue lips or face
